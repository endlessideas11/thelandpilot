✓ Always
  • Use on white, parchment, deep forest green, or near-black
  • Maintain clear space equal to the biplane wingspan on all sides
  • Use the full circular badge — never crop or isolate elements
  • Scale proportionally; minimum size 48px / 0.65in
  • Export SVG for digital; 300dpi PNG for print
— Never
  • Place on busy, patterned, or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Recolor, outline, or alter any element of the badge
  • Add drop shadows, glows, or Photoshop effects
  • Use below minimum size — it will become illegible
  • Stretch, skew, or break the circular boundary

04 — Voice & Messaging

Brand Voice

"Land investing works. But when you're doing it alone — pulling lists, chasing buyers, managing six tools at once — stuff breaks. We built the infrastructure so you don't have to. You answer qualified seller calls and negotiate deals. We handle everything else."

Robin Seib · Founder, The Land Pilot
01
Operator Authority
1,400+ deals. $60M+ in transactions. Speak from volume and experience — never from theory. Numbers do the convincing.
02
Earned Directness
Say exactly what you mean. No jargon without context. No vague promises. Robin admits his million-dollar mistakes in the same breath as his wins.
03
Systems Thinking
Frame everything through infrastructure and process. We're not a coaching program — we're a deal-operating machine that you plug into.
04
Community Warmth
Operators are partners, not clients. Robin remembers names and stories. Anti-hype is a feature, not a constraint. We disqualify bad fits because we care.
Core Positioning
  • Not a coaching program — a deal-operating partner
  • We do deals with you, not just for you
  • Operators, not students; partners, not clients
  • Speed, accuracy, systems, community, scale
  • You negotiate — we handle everything else
Audience — Who This Is For
  • Experienced operators who've done deals and hit walls
  • Investors who want scale, not just their next flip
  • People who answer the phone and close their own contracts
  • Entrepreneurs who think in systems and P&Ls
  • Those who love the land — not just the spreadsheet
Primary Hooks — Proven Language
"Stop building systems — start closing deals."
"Your only two jobs: answer the phone. Show up to calls. Everything else is on us."
"Turn raw land into $20K–$50K+ profits using a system that's closed 1,400+ deals."
"We don't teach you how to open a restaurant. We hand you a McDonald's."
"Six years. A million dollars in trial and error. 30,000 properties comped. You get all of that in 10 days."
Write Like This
  • Lead with concrete numbers — never vague claims
  • Tell stories to make points; never bullet arguments
  • Check in, don't lecture — write like you're talking with a peer, not at a student
  • Operator language: pipeline, underwriting, exit strategy
  • Warmth that comes from respect, not performance
Never Say / Never Do
  • Passive income — this is active, serious business
  • Easy money, shortcuts, or get-rich framing
  • Coaching, course, curriculum, student, mentee
  • Vague testimonials without names and numbers
  • Pressure tactics — the numbers and logic close

Easing
All transitions use a deceleration curve — fast in, slow out. Never bounce. Never spring. This brand does not fidget.
cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
Duration
UI interactions: 160ms. Page transitions: 320ms. Data reveals: 480ms staggered. Never exceed 600ms for functional motion.
160ms · 320ms · 480ms
Principles
Motion signals meaning. Numbers count up on enter. Stats reveal with a sweep. Hover states are immediate. Nothing blinks or pulses without purpose.
